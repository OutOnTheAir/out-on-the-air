Out On The Air is officially live.

If you're reading this, you found the blog — which means you're probably already thinking about your next activation, or maybe you're still figuring out what OOTA even is.

## What is OOTA?

Out On The Air is a logging platform for amateur radio operators who take their rigs outside. Not a contest exchange, not a chase program — just a place to record where you've been and what you've worked, and to let other operators know you're out there.

Self-spotting is encouraged. The log is public. The leaderboard is live.

## What's coming

We're a young platform. The core features are working — activations, QSOs, spots, awards, ADIF import, PDF certificates. But there's a lot more to build, and we're listening to what operators actually want.

If you have a feature request or found a bug, drop a comment on the Reddit thread or email us at [outontheair@outlook.com](mailto:outontheair@outlook.com).

## Get on the air

Register at [outontheair.com](https://outontheair.com), log your first activation, and see where you land on the leaderboard.
